01 · What I study

Making demography spatial

My work spans spatially explicit demographic models, integrated population models, mark‑recapture frameworks, and species distribution models — with a focus on turning demographic inference into evidence‑based management.

Spatial demography

Integrated population models that let survival, productivity, and dispersal vary in space — not just in time — and correct the biases that arise when they don't.

Disease & resilience

Evaluating how emerging wildlife diseases — including the recent HPAI panzootic — reshape demographic rates and long-term population resilience.

Methods & computation

Bayesian hierarchical modelling in JAGS/NIMBLE, simulation studies, and faster computational frameworks for fitting mark-recapture and distance-sampling models at scale.

Conservation applications

Translating demographic inference into insights that are directly usable by managers and conservation practitioners — not just academic outputs.
